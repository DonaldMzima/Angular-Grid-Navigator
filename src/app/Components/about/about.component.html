One Digital Media Programming Challenge

Imagine a grid of city blocks. Given a set of directions, work out how many blocks you would have to walk on the city grid to reach the destination.

You are provided with a list of instructions to navigate on a grid to reach a destination. Start facing north, and then follow the provided sequence: either turn left (L) or right (R) 90 degrees, then walk forward the given number of blocks, ending at a new intersection.

Your task is to work out how far it is from the starting point to the destination, measured on a grid.
